## DESCRIPTION

Handing off a Shopify store is messy. Agencies and freelancers spend hours
hunting down app lists, payment configurations, shipping setups, and theme
details — then formatting them into something a client or incoming team can
actually use.

LaunchGate fixes that in one click.

Install the app, enter any manual credential notes (API keys, DNS logins,
hosting details), and click generate. Within seconds you'll have a
professionally formatted PDF covering everything the new owner needs to
take over the store with confidence.

**What's in the report:**

- Store overview: plan, primary domain, currency, and timezone
- All installed apps and their developers
- Active theme name and publication status
- Payment gateway configuration and supported digital wallets
- Delivery profiles and shipping zone setup
- Custom domains and SSL status
- Store owner contact details
- Your manually entered credential notes — formatted cleanly in their own section

**Who it's for:**

LaunchGate is built for Shopify agencies, freelance developers, and store
consultants who hand off stores to clients or incoming development teams.
It's also useful for store owners doing a brand or ownership transfer, or
anyone who wants a clean record of their store's technical configuration.

**Pricing:**

One-time charge of $29 per store, billed through your existing Shopify
account. No subscription. No recurring fees. Your purchase is valid for
30 days — generate and re-download as many times as you need during that
window.

## NOTES FOR SUBMISSION

- The app uses Shopify's native `appPurchaseOneTimeCreate` billing API.
  No custom payment flow is used.
- In development/test mode, the $29 charge is a Shopify test charge
  (no real money moves). Reviewers can approve it freely.
- GDPR webhooks: customers/data_request, customers/redact, shop/redact
  are all handled. Register the URLs in Partner Dashboard once the
  production URL is confirmed.
- The app is embedded (runs inside Shopify Admin iframe) and uses
  App Bridge v4 + Polaris v13.
