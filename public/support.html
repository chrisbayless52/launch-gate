How to use LaunchGate

  1. 1
    Install the app and open it from your Shopify Admin

    Go to Apps → LaunchGate. You'll see a preview of what's included in your report.

  2. 2
    Add optional credential notes

    Enter any manual notes — API keys, DNS logins, hosting details — that your client will need. These appear in the report but are never stored on our servers.

  3. 3
    Complete the one-time purchase

    Click "Generate handoff report — $29". You'll be taken to Shopify's secure payment page. Approve the charge and you'll return to the app automatically.

  4. 4
    Download your PDF

    Click "Download handoff report". A professionally formatted PDF will download immediately. You can re-download any time within 30 days of purchase.

Frequently asked questions

What data does this app access?
LaunchGate reads store configuration data only — your store name, installed apps, active theme, payment gateways, shipping profiles, custom domains, and store owner email. It never accesses customer records, order history, or product data. All access is read-only; we cannot modify your store. See our full privacy policy for details.
Is this a subscription? Pricing
No. LaunchGate is a one-time charge of $29 per store, billed securely through your existing Shopify account. Your purchase is valid for 30 days, during which you can generate and re-download your report as many times as needed. There are no recurring fees.
Can I generate the report more than once?
Yes. Within 30 days of your purchase you can click "Download handoff report" as many times as you like. Each generation fetches the latest live data from your store, so the report always reflects your current setup.
What if my payment was declined?
Return to the app dashboard in your Shopify Admin and click "Generate handoff report — $29" again. This will start a fresh payment session. If the problem persists, check that your Shopify billing method is up to date under Settings → Billing in your Shopify Admin.
My 30 days expired — do I need to pay again?
Yes. After 30 days the purchase expires and a new one-time payment of $29 is required to generate an updated report. This also ensures your report reflects the current state of the store rather than a stale snapshot.
How do I request deletion of my data?
Uninstalling the app automatically deletes your session data. To request deletion of your purchase record before the 90-day automatic expiry, email support@launchgate.app and we'll remove it within 2 business days.

Need more help?

Email us at support@launchgate.app and we'll get back to you within 2 business days.

Please include your shop domain (e.g. your-store.myshopify.com) in your message so we can look up your account quickly.
