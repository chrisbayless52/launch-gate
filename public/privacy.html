LaunchGate — Privacy Policy

Shopify Handoff Report Generator

Last updated: May 16, 2025  ·  Contact: support@launchgate.app

LaunchGate ("we", "our", "us") is a Shopify app that generates a one-time handoff PDF report of your store's configuration. This policy explains exactly what data we access, what we store, and how long we keep it.

Data we access (read-only)

When you generate a report, we make the following read-only API calls to your Shopify store. We never write, modify, or delete any store data.

  • Store overview — shop name, plan name, primary domain, currency, timezone, and contact email
  • Installed apps — app names and developer names
  • Active theme — theme name and published status
  • Payment configuration — supported digital wallets and payment settings (no card or transaction data)
  • Shipping profiles — delivery profile names and zone counts
  • Custom domains — domain names and SSL status
  • Staff contact — the store owner's email address

All data is fetched at report generation time and used only to produce your PDF. It is not retained in our database after the PDF is generated.

Data we do NOT access or store

  • Customer names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information
  • Order history or transaction amounts
  • Product catalog, inventory, or pricing
  • Discount codes or gift cards
  • Analytics or marketing data
  • Payment card details
  • Any data from your customers' browsers

Data we store in our database

Shopify session token

Required for OAuth authentication. Contains your shop domain and an encrypted access token so you stay logged in. This is standard for all Shopify embedded apps.

Retention: deleted automatically when you uninstall the app (via the app/uninstalled webhook).

Purchase record

When you complete the one-time purchase, we store your shop domain, the Shopify charge ID (a reference number from Shopify's billing system), and the timestamp of payment. This lets us verify you have an active purchase within the 30-day window.

Retention: 90 days from purchase date, then deleted.

Credential notes (optional)

If you type notes into the credential notes field, that text is sent to our server, used to render your PDF, and then immediately discarded. It is never stored in our database.

Data retention summary

Data type Retention Deletion trigger
Session token Until uninstall app/uninstalled webhook
Purchase record 90 days Automatic expiry
Store data (for PDF) Not stored Discarded after PDF generation
Credential notes Not stored Discarded after PDF generation

Third-party services

  • Shopify — authentication, billing, and store data APIs. Governed by Shopify's Privacy Policy.
  • Railway (hosting) — server infrastructure. No customer data is shared; only our application code runs on their infrastructure.

We do not use advertising networks, analytics trackers, or sell data to any third party.

Your rights

You may request deletion of all data we hold for your store at any time by emailing support@launchgate.app. We will respond within 5 business days. Uninstalling the app automatically triggers deletion of your session data via the Shopify GDPR webhook.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and notify you via the Shopify Partner Dashboard notification system. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
